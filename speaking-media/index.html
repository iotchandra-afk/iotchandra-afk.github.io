ORGANIZER BIO

50-word bio

Chandra Kanojia is an enterprise transformation operator who combines Fortune 100 execution accountability with global consulting pattern recognition. He helps large, regulated enterprises connect AI, technology, operating-model change, and adoption to lower cost, shift capacity constraints, and create room for growth.

150-word bio

Chandra Kanojia is an enterprise transformation executive with experience across Fortune 100 operating environments and global consulting. His work sits at the intersection of enterprise AI, platforms, workflow, data, governance, and organizational change. He helps large, regulated businesses unlock trapped operating value by connecting technology investment to the way work, decisions, controls, and adoption actually function. Chandra has led complex transformation across financial services and other regulated environments, bringing together business, product, engineering, data, operations, risk, governance, and change around measurable outcomes. His public speaking has addressed voice and AI in customer engagement, digital cooperation and ethics, healthcare, sustainability, and circular-economy innovation. He holds an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, a B.Tech from the National Institute of Technology Allahabad, a Chief Information Officer Certificate from Carnegie Mellon University, and a BCG Digital Leader Certificate of Completion.
