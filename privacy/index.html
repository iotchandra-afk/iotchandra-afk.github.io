PRIVACY

A minimal-data website.

This site is designed to collect only what is necessary to support a direct conversation.

Contact form

When you submit the contact form, the information you provide is sent through Formspree and delivered to Chandra Kanojia. The form asks for your name, work email, optional organization, and message. It also captures the page, referral, campaign parameters, and submission time so the source of a serious inquiry can be understood.

No mailing list or automated sequence

Your information is not used to add you to a newsletter or automated sales sequence. It is used only to review and respond to your message.

External services

The site may link to LinkedIn, YouTube, event pages, and other public sources. Embedded YouTube media uses the privacy-enhanced youtube-nocookie.com domain. Those services apply their own privacy policies when you interact with them.

Search and indexing

Public pages may be indexed by search engines and AI search services. The site does not publish private submissions or contact-form content.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to chandra@chandrakanojia.com.
